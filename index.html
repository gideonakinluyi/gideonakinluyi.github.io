Artist Statement

The Work Behind the Work

My work explores decision-making, emotional tension, and quiet moments people rarely express. Through symbolic elements like chess and roses, I create pieces that feel deeply personal without explanation. The work comes from a distinctly African perspective, one that holds stillness, dignity, and psychological depth as inseparable from beauty.

Every brushstroke is a move. Every canvas, a board where emotion plays against silence.
