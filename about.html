The Artist

About Gideon

A contemporary painter working at the intersection of psychological storytelling and figurative form, from a distinctly African perspective.

Gideon Akinluyi

Contemporary Artist · Collected Privately Worldwide

Artist Statement

My work explores decision-making, emotional tension, and quiet moments people rarely express. Through symbolic elements like chess and roses, I create pieces that feel deeply personal without explanation. The work draws from a distinctly African identity, one shaped by psychological depth, quiet dignity, and the stories carried in the body before they are ever spoken.

Every brushstroke is a move. Every canvas, a board where emotion plays against silence.

The Work Behind the Work

Each painting begins long before the brush touches the canvas. The ideas form slowly, through observation, through the faces of people carrying stories they will never speak aloud. I am drawn to the moment just before a decision, the quiet before consequence, the stillness that holds everything.

Chess appears throughout my work not as a game but as a language. It speaks of strategy, sacrifice, and the weight of every move. Roses speak of something else entirely, the tenderness that exists alongside difficulty, the beauty that survives pressure. Together, they create a conversation I find endlessly compelling.
